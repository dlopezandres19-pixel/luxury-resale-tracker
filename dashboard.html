Methodology & Notes

Value Retention (VR) = current resale value / original retail price. VR > 100% means the item sells above retail; VR < 100% means depreciation.

Calculation. Per brand, VR is a weighted average by MSRP (handbags) or Retail Price (watches). Chart shows weighted VR per day; table shows latest snapshot with min/max dispersion and sample size (n).

Sample. ~35 handbags across 5 designers (Hermès 10, LV 9, Dior 6, Gucci 6, SL 4). ~150 watches across 4 brands (Rolex, Cartier, Hublot, Piaget). Hublot & Piaget run in fallback mode 🟡 (top-5 by relevance instead of curated icon list).

Geographic coverage. Both sources are US/Western-centric. Does not cover Chinese (Plum, ZZER), Japanese (Komehyo) or Middle Eastern resale markets. Treat values as indicators of Western authenticated resale, not a global benchmark.

Known limitations. Gucci shows a flat line because Foxytotes assigns a single 68% retention to all its Gucci bags (source-side limitation). History starts at first automated run. Scraper runs daily at 23:50 Lima.
